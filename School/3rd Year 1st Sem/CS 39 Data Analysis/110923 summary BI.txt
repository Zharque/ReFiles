Business Intelligence
-BI is a broad category of applications and technologies for gathering, storing, analyzing, and providing access to data to help enterprise users make better business decisions.
-BI technologies provide historical, current and predictive views of business operations.
-BI uses technologies, processes, and applications to analyze mostly internal, structured data and business processes

competitive intelligence 
-gaters, analyzes and disseminates information with a topical focus on company competitors
