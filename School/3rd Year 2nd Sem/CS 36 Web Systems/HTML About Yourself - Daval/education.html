My elementary school years was spent in my hometown at the Lazi Central School.

In Junior High, I studied at the Siquijor Provincial Science High School.

For Senior High, I went to Central Philippine Adventist College-Academy in Murcia, Negros Occidental.

Before transferring to Silliman University, I studied in Accountancy for 1 year in CPAC.

I spent most of my early years participating in MTAP competitions which is why I decided to pursue Accountancy. However I realized that it was not suited for me and so instead I transferred here as a Computer Science student.
